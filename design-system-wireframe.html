Section 00

3-Tier Token Architecture

Why 3 tiers? This architecture separates raw values from meaning from usage. Change a primitive once, and it cascades through semantic and component tokens. Swap themes by remapping semantic tokens. Maintain consistency while enabling flexibility.

Tier 1

Primitive

Raw values — context-free

--kale-3 #173b33
--carrot-2 #f2674b
--charcoal-1 #717377
--space-5 24px

Tier 2

Semantic

Purpose-driven — what it means

--color-text-primary kale-3
--color-action-primary carrot-2
--color-text-secondary charcoal-1
--spacing-component space-5

Tier 3

Component

Specific usage — where it's used

--nav-link-color text-primary
--btn-primary-bg action-primary
--input-placeholder text-secondary
--card-padding spacing-component

Section 08

Art Direction Guidelines

Photography Style

[Dog + Owner Photo]

Lifestyle — Dogs with Owners

Natural light. Warm tones. Real moments (not posed). Focus on connection and joy. Indoor/outdoor home settings.

[Dog Portrait]

Dog Portraits

Eye-level or slightly below. Sharp focus on eyes. Neutral or brand-colored backgrounds. Healthy, happy dogs only.

[Food Photo]

Food Photography

Bright, appetizing. Show real ingredients. Top-down or 45° angle. Fresh, not processed look. Avoid clinical/sterile feel.

Icon Style

[Line Icons]

Line Icons

2px stroke weight. Rounded caps and joins. Kale-2 color. 24×24px base size. Simple, recognizable forms.

[Spot Illustrations]

Spot Illustrations

Flat style with minimal gradients. Brand color palette only. Friendly, not corporate. Used sparingly for emphasis.

[UI Icons]

UI Icons

Solid fill for interactive elements. 16-20px for inline. Charcoal-1 default, Kale-2 for interactive states.

Image Aspect Ratios

16:9

Hero images, video

4:3

Cards, thumbnails

1:1

Avatars, icons

3:4

Portrait photos

21:9

Cinematic banners

Video Thumbnails

▶ [Video Thumbnail]
Play button centered, semi-transparent overlay

Video Standards

16:9 aspect ratio. Centered play button with kale overlay. Thumbnail should convey video content. Captions always available.

Emotional Tone

Warm Trustworthy Premium Approachable Confident Caring Science-backed Not clinical
Brand Voice: We speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporation. We're confident but not arrogant. We use "we" and "you" to create connection. We avoid jargon unless explaining it.
