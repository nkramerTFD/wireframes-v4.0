Talking to your vet?

Our recipes meet AAFCO standards, follow WSAVA guidelines, and are formulated by board-certified veterinary nutritionists. We're happy to share detailed nutritional infojust ask.

AAFCO Complete & Balanced ?
AAFCO (Association of American Feed Control Officials) sets the nutritional standards for pet food in the US. "Complete & Balanced" means every recipe meets minimum requirements for all essential nutrientsprotein, fat, vitamins, and mineralsfor the specified life stage.
WSAVA Guidelines ?
WSAVA (World Small Animal Veterinary Association) provides global guidelines for selecting pet foods. Following these means our food is formulated by qualified nutritionists, undergoes quality control testing, and meets rigorous safety standards veterinarians trust.
Board-Certified Nutritionists ?
Board-Certified Veterinary Nutritionists (DACVN) are veterinarians who completed additional years of specialized training in animal nutrition. They're the same experts your vet consults for complex dietary needs. Every TFD recipe is formulated by these specialists.
